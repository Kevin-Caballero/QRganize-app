Move smarter, not harder.

QRganize helps you pack, label, and find every box during a move — snap a photo, print a QR code, and scan it later to instantly see what's inside.

Get the app See screenshots

Everything you need to pack without losing your mind

📦

Box tracking

Create boxes, snap a photo, and track packing progress with Packing Sealed status.

🏷️

Item details

Log items per box with quantity, fragility, expiration dates, and photos — never forget what's where.

📲

QR labels

Generate and print QR codes for each box, sized for the sheet — scan one to pull up its full contents instantly.

✅

Checklists

Plan what to pack per box; checking an item off automatically adds it as a real item in that box.

🔍

Instant search

Find anything across boxes, items, and checklists by name, description, or contents.

🔔

Expiration reminders

Get a local notification before perishable or time-sensitive items expire.

📡

Works offline

All your data lives on your device — no internet connection required for day-to-day use.

🌗

Light & dark theme

Matches your system theme, or pick Light/Dark manually in Settings.
